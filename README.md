# POF AIO

POF AIO is an Alt1 Player-Owned Farm and Ranch Out of Time assistant for RuneScape 3. It combines animal scanning, farm planning, breeding tools, disease diagnosis, buyer planning, timers, and local record keeping in one application.

## Install

Install it in Alt1 with:

```text
alt1://addapp/https://TrulyBoredAdventure.github.io/POF-AIO/appconfig.json
```

## Tabs

### Dashboard

The Dashboard is the farm command centre. It provides an overview of Manor Farm and the Ranch Out of Time, including saved animals, pen occupancy, breeding-log progress, active timers, food estimates, disease warnings, and other tasks that may need attention. Use the farm selector to view all farms, Manor Farm only, or the Ranch only.

### Scan Animal

Scan Animal reads the open RuneScape Animal Info window through Alt1 and prepares an editable animal record. Review the detected name, breed, gender, growth stage, health, happiness, and traits before saving. The tab also provides an immediate recommendation such as keep for breeding, grow and sell for beans, or sell now for beans. A manual inspection-text importer is included as a fallback.

### Animals

Animals is the saved-animal library. Add, edit, review, protect, or delete individual animals and assign them to pens. Each animal receives a contextual value rating based on its stage, traits, breeding usefulness, saved farm needs, active buyers, breeding-log progress, and estimated bean value.

### Pens

Pens is used to plan and manage the farm layout. Assign saved animals to compatible small, medium, large, breeding, dinosaur, and salamander pens. Track capacity, food type, estimated food duration, farmhands, and farm-totem choices. The tab highlights incompatible animals, full pens, missing food, and other configuration problems.

### Breeding

Breeding compares two selected animals as a breeding pair. Choose a breeding goal and review compatibility, gender and maturity requirements, trait value, shiny potential, special breeding requirements, and suggested improvements. Royal dragon evaluation accounts for the required breath traits.

### Ideal Pairs

Ideal Pairs shows recommended parent trait layouts for each supported species. Filter the recommendations by shiny hunting, breeding output, three-trait progression, low maintenance, or breeding-log variety. The selected goal is aligned with the rest of the app, and each card shows the ideal parent traits plus whether your saved animals already contain the required traits. All recommendations are validated against trait slot and species restrictions.

### Disease

Disease is the Player-Owned Farm disease diagnoser. Select the full symptom descriptions observed when examining the animal's head, eyes, legs and feet, and stomach. The tool ranks possible diseases, identifies the most likely diagnosis, recommends the next body area to examine when the result is uncertain, and displays the appropriate treatment.

### Breeding Log

Breeding Log tracks discovered breeds for Manor Farm and the Ranch Out of Time. Mark normal and shiny breeds as complete, filter the list by farm or species, and use the completion totals to decide which animals or breeding pairs to pursue next.

### Buyers

Buyers helps plan animal sales and bean income. Record the current buyer requests, requested species, preferred breeds or traits, and remaining quantities. The app estimates bean values and uses these buyer records when rating saved or scanned animals.

### Timers

Timers tracks animal growth and other farm reminders. Create timers for stage changes, farm runs, buyers, the trapper, food checks, or any custom task. Due timers appear on the Dashboard and can use browser notifications or sound when enabled.

### Reference

Reference contains searchable information used by the rest of the app, including species, breeds, growth data, valid foods, traits and their slot restrictions, farmhands, and farm-totem perks. Use it to check game information without changing saved farm data.

### Settings

Settings controls notifications and local application data. Copy the Alt1 install address, configure timer lead time and sound, export or import a complete JSON backup, and reset all locally saved POF AIO data. All records are stored locally in the Alt1 browser or the current browser profile.

## Data and Privacy

POF AIO stores animal records, farm plans, scanner corrections, timers, buyers, and settings locally in the Alt1 browser or normal browser profile. Use the JSON export in Settings to create a backup before clearing browser data or moving to another computer.
